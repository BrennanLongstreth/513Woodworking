Delivery

Delivery fees vary by table type as tables vary significantly in weight.
We deliver our tables by carrying them so weight and distance our signifcant factors.
Delivery fees are negotiable based on you providing assistance. But within a few blocks delivery will generally be free
If you would like to pick up your table with a vehicle there is no need to pay for delivery.

Unless another day is requested the tables will be delivered midday on friday.
