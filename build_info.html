Build Information

I build the tables in my backyard using the tools I have on hand.
As I build more tables the quality will increase as I aquire tools and experience.
Images will be added to this website of each table as I build each type.
I do not hold stock of any tables, each table is made to order.
Priming of your order is available.
I DO NOT PAINT TABLES , That is for you and your friends to have fun doing, not for me to do.

Generally speaking I build the tables in a day.
The treated tables take 48 hours to be safe to touch (and will be safe on delivery).
If painting is requested, the table may take more time.
All builds are subject to my workload with school and jobs and may be delayed due to this.

What I will do for your table

  • Treat the bottom of the table legs (regardless of treatment option)
  • Build each table to the best of my ability with the tools I have
  • Lightly round sharp edges

What I will NOT do for your table

  • Paint the top
  • Accept any changes in your order after I have started working
  • Build a table before payment
  • Provide refunds of any kind
terms are subject to change at any time and without notice
